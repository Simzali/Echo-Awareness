Welcome to The Bat Visual Aid

The Bat Visual Aid is an assistive device designed to help people who are visually impaired navigate their surroundings safely and confidently. Using ultrasonic sensors to detect obstacles and a buzzer to provide auditory feedback, The Bat gives users a new level of independence.

Features

  • Dual ultrasonic sensors detect objects up to 90 cm away.
  • Distance-based buzzer feedback for intuitive awareness of surroundings.
  • Modular CAD designs for easy customization of handheld or cane-mounted adapters.
  • Open-source Arduino code for flexible control and community improvement.
