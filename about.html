Our Mission

Our mission is to build technology that empowers the visually impaired by providing safe, intuitive, and affordable solutions for independent navigation. We strive to integrate innovative sensors and feedback systems so users can confidently interact with their environments.

Future Improvements

  • Enhance performance in busy, crowded environments to better detect obstacles.
  • Redesign the device to be slimmer and more compact, making it easier to carry and use daily.
  • Develop a companion app to give users more control and customization options.
  • Add headphone or earbud connectivity for private, discreet auditory feedback.
  • Integrate the system directly into glasses for a seamless and unobtrusive experience.
  • Expand sensing features to include:
    • Color detection for recognizing traffic lights or color-coded signs.
    • Text-to-speech reading using a camera for reading signs and printed text aloud.
    • Object identification through a camera to help users recognize items in their environment.
